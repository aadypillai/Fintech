QUANT COMMITTEE

The Cryptocurrency committee's agenda is intended to be an introductory multi-layered educational program that gives its members the tools necessary to pursue this emerging, highly-disruptive, and widely disregarded financial instrument that, like historically impactful creations of equity securities, debt securities, derivatives, and Forex, has the potential to reshape the financial sector. By the end of the Cryptocurrency program, our members shall have:

  • 1.   Gained applied knowledge on a specific cryptocurrency (Libra, Ethereum, Ripple)
  • 2.   Learned the fundamentals of how cryptocurrencies are exchanged and regulated
  • 3.   Understood a foundational CS side of cryptocurrencies (including but not limited to programs like Solidity.)
  • 4.   brainstormed and created a viable crypto-solution platform

Tentative Schedule

Module One -- Overview of Libra
Module Two -- Cryptocurrency exchange methods and regulations
Module Three -- Fundamentals of Blockchain Programming
Module Four -- Cryptocurrency Project
